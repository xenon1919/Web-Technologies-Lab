Welcome to Matrusri Engineering College

Matrusri Engineering College has been providing quality education since 1981. We are committed to fostering excellence in education and offer a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology.

About Us

Matrusri Engineering College is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and is affiliated with Osmania University. The college has a strong emphasis on research and development and has state-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities.
